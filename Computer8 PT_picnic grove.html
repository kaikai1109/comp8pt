Tagaytay Picnic Grove

The Picnic Grove is not only a place for a quick stop but it's also a tourist spot for having a beautiful view of the Taal Lake and volcano. People would come here for a picnic while enjoying the cool weather.
There are many places where you can take your pictures with the lake itself and its beautiful volcano.
You can buy souvenirs or go on a thrilling ride on the zipline! For only 300 Pesos, you can enjoy this feature like you are flying! It was a fun and scary experience for a student like me! You can also rent and ride a pony to take a stroll while viewing the scenic view of the place.
The surroundings, having plenty of trees and greenery that makes the place all the more fun yet peaceful and calm at the same time. This spot provides a restful stop for tourists who have gone to other nearby adventurous places like we had.
