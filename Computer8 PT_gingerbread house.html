The Gingerbread House

The Gingerbread House is a place filled with colors and numerous enjoyable activities that provides fun for all ages. They sell all kinds of sweet snacks that you could think of such as cotton candy, ice cream, crepes, hot chocolate and more!
They also have game booths and a place where you can leave a message- The Dream Catcher! Some students even tried the fish spa. It was ticklish and fun!
The Gingerbread House also provides educational activities such as inger planting, crepe making demo and gingerbread making and decoration.
In ginger planting, the students were taught how to plant ginger and were given trivia questions about ginger and its benefits to our health.
In crepe making demo, students from grade 7-10 participated in the said activity.
The tour at The Gingerbread House ended with satisfied students with their tummies filled with delicious snacks provided by the tour.
